FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build a website?
Most projects are complete within 5–10 business days from the time we have your content and approval to move forward. Simpler sites can be done faster, and more complex builds may take a bit longer — we’ll always be upfront about the timeline before we start.
Do I need to provide content and images?
You’ll need to provide the text and any photos of your business. If you don’t have photos, we can source high-quality stock imagery that fits your brand at no extra charge.
Will I be able to edit the site myself after launch?
Yes! All our sites are set up with a simple content management system. You can update text, images, hours, and more without any technical knowledge. We walk you through it after launch.
What if I don’t like the initial design?
No worries — that’s what revision rounds are for. We work with you until you love it. Our goal is a site you’re proud to share, and we won’t stop until we get there.
Does the price include hosting?
Your quote covers design and development. Hosting and domain are separate — we’ll recommend the best options for your budget and walk you through the whole setup.
What happens after my support period ends?
You can continue with a monthly maintenance plan, or reach out on a project-by-project basis for updates and changes. We’re always available — the support period just means priority response time.
